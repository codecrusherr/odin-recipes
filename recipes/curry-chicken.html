Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken (cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (chopped, for garnish)

Recipe Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√©ing until they become soft and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the curry powder and cook for an additional minute to release its flavors.
  4. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  6. Cover the pan and let the curry chicken cook for 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with chopped cilantro before serving. Serve over rice or with your favorite side dish. Enjoy the delectable curry chicken!
