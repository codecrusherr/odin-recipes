Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Fresh cilantro

Recipe Instructions

  1. In a large pot or skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until it's fully browned and crumbled.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, cooking until the onion becomes translucent.
  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, chili powder, and cumin. Mix everything well.
  4. Bring the chili to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it cook for about 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Once the chili has thickened and the flavors have melded together, it's ready to serve.
  7. Garnish with shredded cheddar cheese and chopped cilantro if desired. Enjoy your hearty and comforting bowl of chili!
