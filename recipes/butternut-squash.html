Ingredients

  • 1 butternut squash (about 2 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh thyme leaves

Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Peel the butternut squash, remove the seeds, and cut it into 1-inch cubes.
  3. Place the cubed squash on a baking sheet.
  4. Drizzle the olive oil over the squash, ensuring all pieces are coated.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toss the squash on the baking sheet to evenly distribute the oil and seasoning.
  7. Spread the squash in a single layer on the baking sheet for even roasting.
  8. Roast the butternut squash in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until tender and slightly caramelized, flipping the pieces halfway through.
  9. Once done, remove from the oven and garnish with fresh thyme leaves.
  10. Serve as a delightful side dish or use it in your favorite recipes. Enjoy the delicious roasted butternut squash!
