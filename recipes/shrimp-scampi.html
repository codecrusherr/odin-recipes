We're making an shrimp-act!

Let's go scampin'!

Indulge in a tantalizing culinary experience with our mouthwatering Shrimp Scampi recipe. Succulent shrimp sautéed to perfection in a luscious garlic butter sauce, adorned with a burst of zesty lemon, creating a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 cup white wine (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Recipe Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. If using, pour in the white wine and let it simmer for another minute.
  4. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes per side until they turn pink and opaque.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve with lemon wedges on the side. Enjoy!
